FOCUS: Empowering CSE Students for Excellence and Innovation

FOCUS serves as a student governance body for Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) students at the University. Its primary objective is to provide a platform for students to voice their concerns and address them effectively. FOCUS is dedicated to offering opportunities in various academic domains, fostering individual and teamwork capabilities, and promoting excellence in decision-making and perceptual skills among students. Having operated for 18 years, FOCUS has been particularly effective since 2013. The term "FOCUS" symbolizes brilliance, sound decision-making, and students' keen perception abilities. The forum's purpose is to equip students with the essential skills needed to thrive in a rapidly evolving, quality-conscious, and globalized technological landscape. FOCUS provides an ideal platform for students to share ideas, expand their knowledge, realize their potential, and gain valuable experience. In today's technology-driven world, teamwork is crucial, and knowledge is enriched through sharing. FOCUS offers students opportunities to collaborate in teams and discover the true value of teamwork while acquiring knowledge. Additionally, FOCUS organizes National Level Techno Management Fests, providing students with a chance to explore new technologies and insights during a 2 to 3-day break from their academic routines.
